Disclaimer

This application provides automated risk estimates based on a machine learning model trained on historical clinical data. It is intended for demonstration and research review only.

It does not diagnose disease, recommend treatment, or replace consultation with a qualified healthcare professional. Do not use this tool for clinical decision making.

If you have symptoms or concerns about your health, seek advice from a licensed medical provider.

Method

Risk estimates are generated by a Random Forest classifier, selected after comparing six models on the training dataset using cross-validation.
